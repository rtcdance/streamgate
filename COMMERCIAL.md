## FAQ

**Q: Can I use StreamGate in my commercial product?**
A: Yes. MIT license allows unrestricted use, modification, and redistribution.

**Q: Do I need a commercial license?**
A: MIT license is sufficient for self-hosted usage. A commercial license is only needed for
SLA-backed support or priority security patches.

**Q: Is there a hosted version?**
A: Not yet. A managed cloud version is on the [roadmap](product-roadmap.md).
